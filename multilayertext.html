Multilayer Text

Usage: Enter the top text into "top text" and the bottom text into "bottom text".
You can use these characters in top text: "aeioucdhmrtvx". Not every character is supported
Exͪaͫmͫpͫle: Top text: hmmm Bottom text: Example
Jaͥmͣeͫs: Top text: Iam with a space before it Bottom text: James
